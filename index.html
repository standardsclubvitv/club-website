About Bureau of Indian Standards (BIS)

The Bureau of Indian Standards (BIS) is the National Standards Body of India working under the aegis of Ministry of Consumer Affairs, Food & Public Distribution, Government of India. It is established by the Bureau of Indian Standards Act, 2016 which came into effect on 12 October 2017.

BIS has been providing traceability and tangibility benefits to the national economy in a number of ways by:

  • Providing safe reliable quality goods
  • Minimizing health hazards to consumers
  • Promoting exports and imports substitute
  • Control over proliferation of varieties

BIS through its core activities of standardization and conformity assessment has been benefiting the economy by providing safe, reliable and quality goods; minimizing health hazards to consumers; promoting exports and import substitutes; control over proliferation of varieties, etc.

About Our Club

The BIS Standards Club at VIT Vellore was established in 2023 with the mission to create awareness about standardization, quality control, and certification processes among students.

Our objectives include:

  • Promoting awareness about Indian and International standards
  • Organizing workshops and seminars on quality standards
  • Facilitating interaction between students and industry experts
  • Encouraging research in standardization
  • Preparing students for careers in quality management

We believe that understanding and implementing standards is crucial for India's growth as a global manufacturing and service hub. Through our activities, we aim to bridge the gap between academia and industry requirements in the field of standardization.
